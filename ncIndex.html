From Our Family To Yours

Welcome to Cafe In The Park. Whether you are after a quick bite on your bike ride, feeding hungry mouths on a family day out, or looking for somewhere calm and friendly to work we'll make you feel welcome.

Our family have been running a cafe in this beautiful park for over 15 years and we love being at the heart of the local community. Come and sample our delicious food with fresh bakes and a range of seasonal specials.

We can't wait to see you!

"There's always such a warm and relaxed atmosphere and the foot is to die for!"
